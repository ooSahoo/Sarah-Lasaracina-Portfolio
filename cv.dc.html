A bit about me

I’m Sarah — born and based in Bari, on Italy’s sunny heel. I finished my studies and kicked off my career in France before going fully remote during the pandemic, which is probably why I’m happiest switching between languages, cultures and disciplines. I’m a proud cat mom 🐱, my idea of a perfect night out is karaoke or a musical show 🎤, and I only really discovered the joy of cooking when I went meat-free and started moving towards 🌱 — that’s where the clueless cook began. More than anything, in every corner of life I keep coming back to the same belief: how much communication and people’s experience matter to building a society that’s easier to live in and more equitable. Empathy, listening and stepping into other people’s shoes, paired with a genuine interest in technology and understanding innovation — I think that’s the real core of my profile, and it’s what professionally led me to product and UX the long way round, through words.
